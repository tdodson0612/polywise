How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to permanently delete your polywise account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to terryd0612@gmail.com with the subject line:

    "polywise Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your polywise account email address
    • Your full name (optional but helpful)
    • Confirmation that you want the deletion to proceed
  3. You will receive a confirmation reply within 48–72 hours.
  4. Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • Your profile information (name, email, pictures)
  • Your submitted recipes
  • Your uploaded images
  • Your friends list and connections
  • Your scan history

What Data Is Retained Temporarily?

For security and fraud prevention purposes, the following may be retained for up to 30 days:

  • Error logs associated with system reliability
  • Server security logs

These logs contain no personal user data and are automatically purged.

How Long Does Deletion Take?

Data deletion typically completes within 72 hours after verification. Backups containing your data are fully wiped within 30 days.

Need Additional Help?

Email us anytime at terryd0612@gmail.com. We are committed to processing your request promptly and securely.
